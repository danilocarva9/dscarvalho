About

My full name is Danilo de Souza Carvalho, I'm 31 y/o, I was born in Cuiabá, Brazil.

I've been working with software development for almost 10 years. In the beginning I used to work with technologies stacks like APACHE/PHP/MySQL, which helped me a lot to reach some goals in development.
